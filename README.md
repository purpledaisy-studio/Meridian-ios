# Meridian-ios
The public hub and issue tracker for Meridian a  100% offline iOS medical records app built by Purpledaisy
# Welcome to Meridian  (by Purpledaisy)

Hi there! Welcome to the public hub and issue tracker for **Meridian**. 

We got tired of clunky, web-based patient portals and the constant worry about health data breaches. So, we decided to fix it. We built Meridian: a beautifully designed, **100% offline iOS medical records app**. 

## Who are we?
Meridian is the  app built by [Purpledaisy](https://bypurpledaisy.com), a privacy-first iOS development studio. We believe your private data should actually stay private. 

## The "Zero Cloud" Rule
Your health data is the most sensitive information you own. That's why Meridian is built differently from the big hospital apps:
* **No servers, no accounts:** Your data never leaves your iPhone. 
* **Smart On-Device Scanning:** Instead of sending your documents to a server, we use built-in **Apple VisionKit OCR** to instantly read and organize your lab results right in the palm of your hand.
* **Your Private Health Records:** You own your data. Period.
